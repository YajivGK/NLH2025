About the Hackathon

A legal hackathon is a collaborative event where lawyers, technologists, and other professionals come together to create innovative solutions to legal challenges. These events can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, with participants working in teams to brainstorm and develop new ideas and prototypes. Legal hackathons foster a dynamic environment for cross-disciplinary collaboration, encouraging creative problem-solving and the development of technology-driven solutions. They also offer a valuable opportunity to network, exchange knowledge, and help shape the future of the legal industry through innovation and teamwork. These events empower participants to drive innovation in the legal sector, promoting fresh perspectives and cutting-edge solutions. They also provide a platform to showcase talent, build connections, and stay ahead in an evolving industry.

About Department of Computer Technology

Department of Computer Technology is located in the MIT Campus, Anna University and functioning under the Faculty of Information and Communication Engineering. This Department is offering Under Graduate programme B.E. Computer Science and Engineering, Post Graduate programme M.E. Computer Science and Engineering and Doctoral programme Ph.D. The department is committed to the preparation of graduates to address the great challenges in the field of Information Technology and to contribute for research and innovation that ensure the sustained growth in Computing. Since 2018, this department follows UG Curriculum, based on Centrally Sponsored Scheme known as Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA). The courses are designed to combine rigorous academic study and exhaustive practical exposure for students with in-depth lab oriented courses, projects, and internships. Students can have their degree with Honors by doing additional courses of their choices from critical domains. The faculty members adopt digital pedagogy to attain the best quality of education.

About TNDALU

The University is the first full-fledged sui generis affiliating Law University in the country; it is unique among all the Law Universities in the Country. It offers Legal Education through this University and its affiliated colleges in the State and the University is the first of its kind not only in the country but also in South Asia. The University offers legal education at its campus as well as through fifteen affiliated government law colleges in the state of Tamil Nadu .University offers legal education through nine private law colleges located in the different parts of Tamil Nadu. The School of Excellence in Law was established by the University in the year 2002 at its campus has been modeled on the lines of other National Law Schools in the country and offers three year as well as five year integrated undergraduate courses in Law.
